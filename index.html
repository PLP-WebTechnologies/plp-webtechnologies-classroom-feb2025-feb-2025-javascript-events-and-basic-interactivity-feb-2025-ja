Our Journey

Founded in 2009, Prestige Motors began as a modest dealership specializing in compact vehicles. Our founder, Denzel Odhiambo, started with a single showroom and a vision to transform Kenya's automotive landscape.

Over the past 15 years, we've evolved from selling small cars to becoming Nairobi's premier luxury automotive destination. Our growth has been driven by an unwavering commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and the pursuit of automotive perfection.

Today, we stand as a testament to Kenyan entrepreneurship, offering an exclusive collection of the world's finest automobiles. Our journey from a small dealership to a luxury automotive empire reflects our dedication to quality and our deep understanding of the evolving needs of our distinguished clientele.
